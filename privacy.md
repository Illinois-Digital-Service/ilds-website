      <h2 class="label">PRIVACY POLICY</h2>
      <p class="lead">Illinois Digital Service is committed to protecting your privacy.</p>

      <h3>Scope</h3>
      <p>This Privacy Policy (Policy) describes how Illinois Digital Service treats personal information on our website where the Policy is located (Platform). Please note that this Policy applies only to Illinois Digital Service and does not apply to other entities.</p>

      <h3>Types of Information We Collect</h3>
      <p>We collect information from you and about you. Here are some examples of the information we may collect:</p>
      <ul class="privacy-list">
        <li>
          <strong>Contact Information</strong>
          <p>For example, we may collect your name, mailing address, telephone number, and email address.</p>
        </li>
        <li>
          <strong>Information You Submit</strong>
          <p>We may collect any information that you submit on the Platform. For example, if you submit an application or inquiry, we may collect information about your interests, occupation, and other information.</p>
        </li>
        <li>
          <strong>Device Information</strong>
          <p>For example, we may collect the type of device you use to access our Platform. We may also collect your device identifier, IP address, or mobile operating system.</p>
        </li>
        <li>
          <strong>Other Information</strong>
          <p>If you use our website, we may collect information about the browser you are using. We might look at what site you came from, or what site you visit when you leave us.</p>
        </li>
      </ul>

      <h3>How We Collect Your Information</h3>
      <p>We collect your information in different ways. Below are some examples of how we may collect your information.</p>
      <ul class="privacy-list">
        <li>
          <strong>Directly From You</strong>
          <p>For example, when you interact with our social media accounts or submit information to us through our website.</p>
        </li>
        <li>
          <strong>Passively</strong>
          <p>For example, when you visit and navigate our Platform on any device. We may also collect information about users over time and across different websites and devices when you use the Platform. For example, we may use Google Analytics on our Platform to obtain insights about traffic to our Platform and similar uses.</p>
        </li>
        <li>
          <strong>From Third Parties</strong>
          <p>We may receive information about you from other sources. For example, this may include receiving information from our partners, including analytics vendors or philanthropic partners. It may also include receiving information from our affiliates.</p>
        </li>
        <li>
          <strong>By Combining Information</strong>
          <p>For example, we may combine information that we collect offline with information we collect through our Platform. We may also combine information we collect about you from the different devices you use to access our Platform.</p>
        </li>
      </ul>

        </li>
        <li>
          <strong>For Security Purposes</strong>
          <p>This could include protecting our organization and visitors to our Platform.</p>
        </li>
        <li><strong>As Otherwise Permitted By Law or As We May Notify You</strong></li>
        <li><strong>As Requested or Directed By You</strong></li>

          <p>We may share your information with our parent, subsidiary, and affiliate entities.</p>
        </li>
        <li>

      <p>If you are a parent or legal guardian and think your child under 13 has provided us with information, please <a href="{{ '/connect/' | relative_url }}" class="link-inline">contact us</a>. Please mark your inquiries "COPPA Information Request."</p>

      <p>You have certain choices about how we use your information. Certain choices you make are browser and device specific.</p>
      <ul class="privacy-list">
        <li>
          <strong>Marketing Communications</strong>

        </li>
        <li>
          <strong>Cookies</strong>

        </li>
        <li>

          <p>We may use Google Analytics on our Platform to help us understand how you use our Platform. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the <a href="https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="link-inline">Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on<span class="visually-hidden"> (opens in new tab)</span></a>.</p>
        </li>

If you block cookies, certain features on our site may not work. If you block or reject cookies, not all of the tracking described here will stop.</p>
